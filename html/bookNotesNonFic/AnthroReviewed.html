🚀 The Book in 3 Sentences

1. In this book, John Green reviews random concepts from the beautiful sunsets to the Pigly Wigly stores.
2. Packed in these small tiny reviews is so much wisdom and hope that I can’t get enough of it. John Green views the world in such a positive manner, that it is difficult for me to see it otherwise.
3. He talks about Hand paintings in caves, sunsets, the song - “You’ll never walk alone”, among many other things.
4. Honestly this is a love letter to human kind.

🎨 Impressions

I really loved the way Green explains how meaningful certain things are and it makes me want to appreciate things that are meaningful to me too.

How I Discovered It

This is literally a best seller and was very popular when it came out, which made me want to read it.

Who Should Read It?

Anyone who wants a more positive and hopeful outlook to life.

☘️ How the Book Changed Me

  • Remember this word, “Believe” it has both be and live in it
  • The book is making me look at the world with wonder and hope.
